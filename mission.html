Mission Statement

Our Mission

To preserve, promote and disseminate all forms of art and culture of India through a series of well-planned initiatives and frameworks. We aim to promote cultural heritage and encourage cultural understanding between various regions, creating a harmonious cultural environment in the country.

"Preserving our rich cultural heritage while fostering innovation and creativity for future generations"

Key Objectives

  • Preserve and promote India's rich cultural heritage through various initiatives and programs
  • Support and encourage cultural activities at various levels
  • Maintain and conserve ancient monuments and historical sites
  • Promote cultural awareness and understanding among the youth
  • Foster cultural exchanges at both national and international levels

Our Commitment

We are committed to protecting, preserving, and promoting India's cultural heritage in all its rich diversity. Through various schemes and programs, we work towards making cultural activities accessible to all sections of society while ensuring the preservation of our invaluable cultural assets for future generations.
