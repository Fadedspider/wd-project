About Ministry of Culture

Overview

The Ministry of Culture plays a vital role in preserving and promoting India's rich cultural heritage. We are dedicated to the preservation, promotion, and dissemination of all forms of art and culture in India. Through various initiatives and programs, we work to maintain and nurture our country's diverse cultural fabric.

Our mandate encompasses a wide range of cultural activities, from maintaining ancient monuments to promoting fine arts, from preserving traditional folk arts to encouraging cultural exchanges. We work closely with various organizations and institutions to ensure that India's cultural heritage is preserved for future generations.
