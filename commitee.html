Gandhi Heritage Portal Committee

The National Salt Satyagraha Memorial, Dandi, Gujarat, India

The Mission will function through Committees which will supervise, guide and assist in conservation initiative, maintenance, or preservation initiative and the creation of a database of tangible, literary and visual Gandhi Heritage. Committees as recommended by the Panel have been constituted which are as follows;

i. Conservation Committee chaired by Shri Kartikeya Sarabhai.

ii. Archival Committee chaired by Prof. Shahid Amin.

iii. Gandhi Heritage Sites Database Committee chaired by Dr. Sudarshan Iyengar.

iv. Museum Committee chaired by Shri Anupam Mishra.

v. A Finance Committee has also been constituted which will be headed by Additional Secretary & Financial Advisor, Ministry of Culture.
